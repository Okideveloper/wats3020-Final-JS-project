Best beaches in Okinawa, Japan

By Amanda Teague

Manza Beach "Located at ANA Intercontinental Manza Beach Resort with many water rentals including access to the large and popular playground on the water".

Okuma Beach "Located on a military base you will have camping, grilling and fun in the water with many beach activities including kayaks and jet skis."
Kerama Beach "This is the beach where you can swim with the sea turtles and the many beautiful colorful fish. You will need a ferry to reach this beach."
Sesoko Beach "No camping here but you can snorkel and relax at this 800-meter-long white smooth sandy beach with crystal-clear water. Located at Sesoko 73 Kunigamigun Motobucho."
Emerald Beach "Located within Okinawa Ocean Expo Park in Motobu-cho. Relax and enjoy the sun with no admission fees."
Zanpa Beach "Located just before Cape Zanpa, with plenty of water activites. Feel the sand between your toes! Pure white sand, beautiful blue water with no rocks to step on."

Interested in swimming with Sea Turtles? That's right! Get your snorkeling gear and take a dive.
